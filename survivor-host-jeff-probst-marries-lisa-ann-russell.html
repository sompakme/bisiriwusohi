Survivor Host Jeff Probst Marries Lisa Ann Russell!

Publish date: 2024-07-05

Their love will survive!

A CBS rep confirms to Us Weekly that Jeff Probst and Lisa Ann Russell were married in L.A. Monday. The couple exchanged vows in front of family and friends in an intimate ceremony.

This is the second marriage for both stars. Probst, 50, ended his five-year union with Shelley Wright in 2001. Russell, 40, was married to Saved By the Bell's Mark-Paul Gosselaar, 38, for 15 years until May 2011. They have two children together: son Michael, 7, and daughter Ava, 5.
