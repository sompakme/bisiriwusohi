Who Is V Spehar On TikTok? His Real Name And Wikipedia

V Spehar is a digital creator well-known for his Current events TikTok segment, Under The Desk News.

Created the show in January 2021, he covers a wide variety of topics from politics, law, local affairs, and pop culture and shares with his followers that count to more than 2 million.

Though not on Wikipedia, V Spehar has a short bio on his website.

Before coming to fame, he had a long career in the hospitality industry. From dishwashing to directing James Beard Foundation, he has done it all.

A founding board member of the Queer Food Foundation and Rainbow Families, he wants to create diversity and inclusivity for the LGBTQ+ community within the industry.

Likewise, he previously held the position of Everything Food’s Executive Director of Impact, Hungry Harvest’s Director of Sales & Partnerships, and Design Cuisine’s Director of New Business & Marketing Account Executive.

As for the TikToker’s real name, it is probably Vitus, as per his official website. Moreover, his pictures suggest he is in his late twenties to mid-thirties.

Did TikTok Star V Spehar Died? Death And Crossword Clue Explained

TikTok star V Spehar hasn’t died.

His most recent Under The Desk News dropped a few hours ago today. It was about the judges’ decision in the United Kingdom to label calling a man bald a form of sexual harassment. He tried to utter the news in a British accent.

With that, the likelihood of his death doesn’t seem plausible. However, his 60-second current news TikTok segment made its way to the Daily Crossword section of The Los Angeles Times on Saturday, 14 May 2022.

It was a clue for the #34 Across section of the puzzle. The hint says that he broadcasts the news segment from his office floor.

Many crossword solvers named UNDERTHEDESKNEWS as the most probable word.

So, the TikToker is still alive, but the death rumors remain.
