Riverdale, the CW drama series brings to light great young talent within its seasons set in a dark, crime-filled small town. The show is based on the Archie comics characters as they make their way through life and high school. Add to that, the mysterious death of a student.

The main character (Archie Andrews) and his friends try to find the missing pieces at the same time dealing with their personal life issues. We know you’re probably wondering what romantic escapades your favorite characters' actors are involved in away from the camera. Here are Riverdale’s main characters and their current relationship statuses.

K.J. Apa (Archie)

The New Zealand actor, Keneti James Fitzerald “KJ” Apa who plays Archie Andrews in the Riverdale series has been quite mysterious about his love life. Unlike his co-stars, he keeps a low romantic profile.

Though the young actor has never confirmed being in a relationship, there have been Instagram photos of him and Corinne Isherwood, a Canadian model, at photoshoots arm in arm. In 2017, he told Cosmopolitan that he was waiting for the right time and that his present engagements were taking so much of his time. The actor has also been linked to Britt Robertson, a co-star from A Dog’s Purpose, but no official confirmation has been given by the actor that the two are an item, so at the moment, he is single.

Lili Reinhart (Betty)

The 23-year-old American actress who plays the role of Betty Cooper in Riverdale has officially been confirmed to be dating co-star, Cole Sprouse. Though they were rumored to have broken up, sources have it that the two are still an item and Reinhart posted some make-out pictures of them on Instagram to confirm that. The reports are that the two of them are very happy and get along quite well. Lili made it quite clear they never broke up and showed her discontent with the false rumors that had been spreading earlier about a break, with Sprouse echoing her sentiments.

Camila Mendes (Veronica)

The American Actress who plays Veronica Lodge in Riverdale has reportedly broken up with her once-boyfriend, Charles Melton. The two co-stars dated for over a year before putting an end to the relationship. Mendes confirmed she and Charles were dating through an Instagram post back in 2018, after being spotted out together a few times. After just six months of dating, Melton showed off his ‘Cami’ tattoo. The two ex-lovers were also together at the 2019 Met Gala and celebrated their first anniversary in August this year. The two officially confirmed they were taking a break from their relationship, not giving any reason in particular but that they were simply overwhelmed with work and needed time to focus on work and themselves.

Cole Sprouse (Jughead)

The American actor who plays Jughead Jones in Riverdale, and is also a recipient of the Teen Choice Award for Choice Male Hottie is currently in a romantic relationship with co-star, Lili Reinhart. The two have been dating for two years now and came out in November 2019 to put an end to rumors that the two had broken up in July 2019. Both Lili and Sprouse confirmed they are still an item and that they are happy and getting along very well. Lili also took to her Instagram to celebrate her partner winning the award of Drama Movie Star of 2019 for his role in Five Feet Apart and also winning the Male TV Star of 2019 for Riverdale.

Madelaine Petsch (Cheryl)

Madelaine Grobbelaar Petsch is the 25-year-old American actress who plays Cheryl Blossom. She is currently in a romantic relationship with Travis Mills. The two maintain a long-distance relationship and Madelaine has been quite public about her relationship with Mills.

Travis took to his Instagram to share about how the two managed to maintain their long-distance relationship and also shared some of his thoughts concerning ghosting each other, and how the two have managed to keep in touch and avoid things detrimental to their relationship. The two have been seen many times in public including their appearances at the 2018 Teen Choice Awards, the 2018 Emmys, and once Travis made a guest appearance on Madeleine's YouTube channel.

Ross Butler (Reggie)

Ross Butler is an American actor. The 29-year old actor played the role of Reggie Mantle in the first season of the CW series Riverdale, before relinquishing the role to star in 13 Reasons Why. Ross is reportedly not dating anyone at the moment, though the actor has revealed he is actually searching. He also makes it clear he believes his schedule could actually be a hiccup for him should he find a romantic partner. The star isn’t giving that much attention to the dating scene, and though he hasn’t totally put it away, the actor seems more focused on fulfilling roles in his career.

Vanessa Morgan (Toni)

This Canadian actress is the winner of the Teen Choice Award for Choice Scene Stealer and Teen Choice Award for Choice Breakout TV Show (both for her role in Riverdale). She plays the role of Toni Topaz in the CW series. The award-winning actress became engaged to baseball’s White Sox star, Michael Kopech, in July this year, so this one is off the market for all of you looking to make a move on the 27-year-old. Vanessa took to her Instagram page to share the good news and was congratulated by her fans as well as fellow cast members.

Jordan Connor (Sweet Pea)

Jordan Connor is a Canadian actor best known for his role as Sweet Pea in the CW TV series Riverdale. The 28-year-old has also made appearances in other notable TV series including the CW's Supernatural, and other shows like Lucifer, No Tomorrow, and The Tomorrow People. Jordan got engaged to his girlfriend, Jinjara Mitchel, in September 2018. At the time he shared the new development on his Instagram account, Connor revealed that asking Jinjara to marry him was the most important question he had ever asked. Connor was also met with a flood of congratulations from fellow cast members from Riverdale and fans.

Hart Denton (Chic)

Hart Denton plays the role of Chic in the CW TV series Riverdale. The 26-year-old American actor’s romantic life isn’t quite clear at the moment. The young man is also an Instagram sensation and a model who is signed to NEXT Model Management was in a long time relationship with fellow model Sunny Soofiani. Both Hart and Sunny were very open about their relationship, as was shown by their multiple Instagram posts and other information shared on social media. However, for a prolonged period now neither of the two has posted about each other so it appears that the pair have parted ways.

Casey Scott (Kevin)

Casey Scott is best known for his role as Kevin Keller in the CW series. In the original comic his character is the first openly gay person in the Archie comics universe. The reception for the new arrival, which appeared in issue #202 of Veronica, was so massive that Archie comics had to run a reprint for the first time in its history. Scott is a 26-year-old American actor who portrays the character in the TV series. Scott is currently not dating anyone, and in February this year, the actor revealed that he is enjoying the single life. The actor was previously linked to actress Stephanie Styles but the two have split up.
