Andrew Dismukes Girlfriend: Who Is Aly Dixon?

Andrew Dismukes and Aly Dixon began dating in August 2016.

Aly Dixon is a comedian and writer based in Los Angeles, Baby.

She was born on February 10, 1998, in Boston.

Dixon graduated from Ithaca College with a bachelor’s degree in writing.

She stands at a height of 5 feet 6 inches tall.

Aly began comedy with the Austin scene before moving to LA in 2017.

She has performed at several festivals including Comedy Central’s Clusterfest, Austin Sketch Fest, Orlando Indie Comedy Fest, Out of Bounds Comedy Fest, Del Close Marathon, et al.

Aly trained at ColdTowne Theater, iO Chicago, and Pack Theater.

She performs monthly with her Pack House Sketch Team, Gutter as well as hosts a monthly standup showcase, Pregnancy Pact.

Aly Dixon contributed to satirical website Reductress; writing and co-hosting the famous original web series Hit Play; producing, writing and hosting the quirky, pop culture web series Millennially Challenged.
