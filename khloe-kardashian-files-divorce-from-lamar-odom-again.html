Khloe Kardashian Files Divorce From Lamar Odom Again

Publish date: 2024-08-15

While some of us want the old thing back, Khloe Kardashian is ready to move on to bigger and better things. The reality star and herbal-tea-lover has filed for divorce once again from former Laker Lamar Odom.

According to E! Online, the business woman filed the papers on Thursday (May 26) citing ‘irreconcilable differences.” Khloe remained by Odom’s side while he was recovering from a drug overdose back in October 2015. His health troubles were reportedly the reason why she decided to dismiss her 2013 divorce petition and wait until he was back on his feet to move forward with her decision. Odom also agreed with the reasons behind the divorce.

The two met in August 2009 and married a month later. Their romance and wedding were televised in the program, Khloe & Lamar. Highlights included their joint unisex fragrance and Odom’s bromance with Rob Kardashian.

News of her divorce isn’t entirely surprising. The 31-year-old has posted cryptic messages on social media about coming to terms with her divorce, a notion she was strongly against.
