Stone Cold Praises John Cena and Paul Heyman on Raw, New Stable Loses Dark Match, Final Raw Rating

Publish date: 2024-08-04

Stone Cold Praises Cena and Heyman

Many former and current wrestlers and personalities have praised the promo work between John Cena and Paul Heyman from Raw on Monday. WWE Hall of Famer “Stone Cold” Steve Austin tweeted the following about the opening of Raw:

@HeymanHustle serves up a tremendous promo for @JohnCena on #RAW. Cena returns fire with an A-1 promo. Great work by both guys. #wwe

— Steve Austin (@steveaustinBSR) July 29, 2014

New Stables Loses Match

Kofi Kingston and Big E, with Xavier Woods at ringside, lost a dark match in between the WWE Main Event and Smackdown tapings to Stardust and Goldust last night.

Final Raw Rating

The final rating for WWE Raw was a 3.06, which is down from last week’s 3.15 television rating. The show drew 4.318 million viewers, also down from last week.
