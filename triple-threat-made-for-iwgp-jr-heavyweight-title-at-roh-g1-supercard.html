Triple Threat Made For IWGP Jr. Heavyweight Title At ROH G1 Supercard

Publish date: 2024-07-24

Ring Of Honor (ROH) has just announced a huge Triple Threat match for the G1 Supercard. Taiji Ishimori will defend his IWGP Jr. Heavyweight Championship against Bandito and Dragon Lee:

BREAKING: @LuchadorBandido will compete in a three-way match against @taiji_ishimori and @dragonlee95 for the IWGP Junior Heavyweight Championship at #G1Supercard @TheGarden!! #ROH vs #NJPW vs #CMLL

Sat Apr 6 – 730 ET – LIVE on PPV & FREE for #HonorClub! @njpwglobal pic.twitter.com/5KNFfGerfK

— ROH – Ring of Honor Wrestling (@ringofhonor) March 7, 2019

Only two other matches are confirmed for the pay-per-view (PPV) as of this writing. Jay White will defend his IWGP Heavyweight Championship against the New Japan Cup winner. Also, the IWGP Tag Team Titles and ROH World Tag Team Titles will be on the line when The Guerillas Of Destiny (Tama Tonga and Tanga Loa) take on The Briscoe Brothers (Jay and Mark Briscoe).

The G1 Supercard takes place from Madison Square Garden in New York City, New York, on April 6, 2019.
