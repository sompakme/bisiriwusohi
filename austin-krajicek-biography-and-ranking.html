Austin Krajicek Biography And Ranking

Publish date: 2024-09-15

Austin Krajicek is a famous American professional tennis player. He is a distant cousin of Dutch tennis player and former Wimbledon winner Richard Krajicek, whose half-sister Michaëlla Krajicek is also a tennis player. He played college tennis at Texas A&M.

Austin Krajicek was born on June 16, 1990, in Tampa, Florida, in the United States of America. There is no information about his parents, siblings, and early childhood life.

Austin Krajicek is currently a student of the Texas A&M University where he plays tennis.

Before 2018, Austin Krajicek competed mainly on the ITF Futures and ATP Challenger Tour, both in singles and doubles.

Austin Krajicek made his World TeamTennis debut in 2020 with the Orange County Breakers at The Greenbrier.
