Heres What Khloe Kardashian Spends Her Money On

Publish date: 2024-08-09

When it comes to spending her millions, Khloe Kardashian does not shy away. As we’ll see throughout the article, her recent spending habits have a lot to do with daughter True. She’s pretty darn spoiled, with a playhouse worth more than some of our cars, not to mention a toy Bentley ride which in truth (no pun intended), looks better than most of our cars! Despite the spending habits on her daughter, as we’ll see throughout the entries, she isn’t apologetic about it.

We’ll take a look at her other spending habits, some more expensive than others, like her beautiful home worth over $7 million, along with one luxurious Rolls Royce SUV. Not everything she buys has a seismic cost attached, we’ll also identify her modest spending habits, like a pair of cheap hoops from Claire’s valued at $10.

Enjoy the article folks, let’s take a look at what Khloe Kardashian spends her money on.

11 True’s $12,500 Playhouse

A lot of Khloe’s bank account is going towards her daughter True nowadays. Fans took exception to her ways, spoiling her daughter on the norm. However, Khloe wasn’t having it, defending the fact that it doesn’t change True’s values, even if she has a near $13,000 playhouse in her backyard, one similar to Kylie’s;

"Yes, I do choose to spoil my daughter. I want to spoil her with love as well with material items. She will also be raised with values, responsibilities, chores, respect, self-love. We all work hard and we are able to spend our money in the way that we choose. I hope that's what comes across to 'the outside world', To lead with love above anything else."

10 True’s Toy Bentley

We know this was an expensive purchase when even the likes of Floyd Mayweather, who is close to billionaire status bought one for his kid. Once again, Khloe had to face the wrath of certain fans for her spending habits – this time though, she clapped back against a negative comment on IG, here’s what she had to say;

“Ummm you know this is not a real Bentley? It’s a toy. No reason to be sad over a baby in a toy car. Either way I’m sorry you feel that way but I hope you have a great weekend! Focus on things that make you happy! Smile.”

9 $250K New Rolls Royce SUV

Khloe always rides in style and as we’ve seen in the past, her go-to in terms of rides seems to be anything Rolls Royce.

She purchased the pricey SUV version, which is valued at a near quarter of a million. Khloe was seen in the ride leaving a filming studio over in Agoura Hills, California. It was a heart-racing moment for both her and the fans, as she struggled to get out of the parking lot, nearly crashing the new ride. That would not have been good.

8 Buying Justin Bieber’s $7.2 Million Mansion

She dropped over $7 million on this stunning home, located in The Oaks, a closed-off and gated community in Calabasas. According to Forbes, the home was owned by a few celebs, including Justin Bieber, along with the former couple of Kaley Cuoco and Ryan Sweeting.

The home is absolutely stunning, and it also features a couple of huge garages – not a surprise, she converted the garage into a gym – we’ve seen her work a sweat alongside her sisters and Tristan, in the comfort of her own home.

7 Recent Good Deeds For The Elderly

During these tough times, the Kardashians have been doing their absolute best when it comes to giving back. Sister Kim broke the bank, donating $3.6 million in the support of mothers and children.

Khloe would also follow suit, heading out in LA and offering to buy the groceries for the elderly. A kind act of generosity, Khloe doesn’t shy away from opening her wallet when it comes to making charitable donations or helping those in need.

6 R-Rated Gifts To Mom

Khloe has a big sense of humor when it comes to gifting certain items to family members. That was especially the case when it came time for Mother’s day, as she made some hilarious choices when it came to gifts for Kris Jenner.

Along with some alcoholic relief, Khloe purchased some R-rated gifts we can’t discuss in length. Jenner loved the gifts and she shared all of them via IG; "I'm opening Khloe's Mother's Day gift and we're off to a good start." Her words after unwrapping the bottle of Belvedere.

5 DASH Investment

Khloe made a few risky investments in the past. One of her first big purchases was the DASH boutique, which took a while before becoming profitable. She discussed the risk involved with Cheat Sheet;

“We literally had no money. The three of us bought all our own inventory, cleaned the store, did our own taxes – everything. There were definitely months where we couldn’t pay our bills. The store was just breaking even for six or seven years! We didn’t make any money, and it was really scary.”

4 Kardashian Beauty Investment Gone Wrong

Some investments turn into a major headache, no matter who you are. That was the case for Khloe and her sisters, as they were slapped with a massive $200 million lawsuit, according to Cheat Sheet.

This came off a problematic loan from Capital Management, worth $10 million due to the company’s struggles. At least we know that at times, what the family does isn’t always going to be a massive success.

3 Hew Own App

All the sisters would also get involved in the app business – they used a subscription model in order to be profitable. According to Variety, the sisters did make a decent profit, despite the large investment. However, after three years of updating the app, they all mutually pulled the plug on it;

“We’ve had an incredible experience connecting with all of you through our apps these past few years but have made the difficult decision to no longer continue updating in 2019. We truly hope you’ve enjoyed this journey as much as we have, and we look forward to what’s ahead.”

2 $200K To Get Rid Of Tristan’s Purchases

This was basically a renovation to remove anything Tristan related, from photos to even the things that he touched. According to The Mirror, it was Khloe’s way of being in control of the situation.

The entire renovation was a costly one, worth a near $200,000. As far as Khloe goes, it was worth every penny, essentially symbolizing a new start.

1 Modest Spending For Certain Jewelry

For certain things, Khloe has moderate spending habits just like the rest of us. Turns out, she bought her hoop earrings at a cheaper price point for under $10 at Claire’s. Here’s what she had to say about the purchase via Twitter;

“I got my really big ones at Nine West. I only buy inexpensive hoops because I loose them often. The rest are from Claire’s. They’re so good and under $10.”
