Is Clementine Ford Cybill Shepherds Daughter? Does Cybill Shepherd have grandchildren?

Publish date: 2024-07-10

Clementine Ford is Cybill Shepherd’s daughter. Clementine Ford was born to actress Cybill Shepherd and her first husband, David Ford, in Memphis, Tennessee in 1979.

Cybill Shepherd became a grandma for the first time in 2014. Her daughter Clementine Ford gave given birth to a baby boy named Elijah. Her second grandchild, Welles Molavi Ford-Wilcox, was born to Clementine on September 13, 2016.

Cybill Shepherd appeared alongside Jennifer Love Hewitt in the 2010 television film, The Client List as Cassie and was subsequently cast as Linette Montgomery in the 2012-13 series based on the film.

In July 2012, Cybill Shepherd made her Broadway debut in the revival of Gore Vidal’s The Best Man at the Gerald Schoenfeld Theatre.

Cybill Shepherd took on the role of a mother grieving the death of her daughter in Do You Believe? (2015), a Christian-themed movie.

In 2023, Cybill Shepherd starred in the Lifetime film How to Murder Your Husband: The Nancy Brophy Story, where she portrayed Nancy Brophy, opposite Steve Guttenberg.

Cybill Shepherd was cast as Colleen Champion in NBC’s critically acclaimed television drama The Yellow Rose (1983), opposite Sam Elliott.
