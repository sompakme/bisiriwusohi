Pauly D Ranked One of the Worlds Highest Paid DJs!

Publish date: 2024-09-16

According to Forbes, Pauly D is one of the highest paid DJs in the world. The magazine dropped the list last week via its website and the MTV reality star sits at number seven with $11 million earned in the past year. Besides his stint on the critically acclaimed Jersey Shore, Pauly D involvement in his pre-game cocktail REMIX, and deal with 50 Cent’s G-Note Records helps him fly above his ‘Shore’ cast mates.

Sitting slightly above Pauly D is his one-time rival, Deadmau5 with $11.5 million earned in the last year. Tiesto claims the number one spot with $22 million earned. Check out the full list below.

1. Tiesto – $22 million
2. Skrillex – $15 million
3. Swedish House Mafia – $14 million
4. David Guetta – $13.5 million
5. Steve Aoki – $12 million
6. Deadmau5 – $11.5 million
7. DJ Pauly D – $11 million
8. Kaskade – $10 million
9. Afrojack – $9 million
10. Avicii – $7 million
