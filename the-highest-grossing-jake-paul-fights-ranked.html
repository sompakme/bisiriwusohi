Jake Paul is a famous American social media personality and professional boxer. Jake is 26 years old and a little brother to Logan Paul who is also a social media personality. He initially rose to fame on Vine, with over a million followers. Jake Paul's net worth is estimated to be about $38 Million. He is best known for playing the role of Dirk Mann on the Disney Channel series Bizaardvark (2016) for two seasons.Paul's behavior has been the source of numerous controversies throughout his career.

He got involved in risky stunts, sexually suggestive behavior, and featuring age-inappropriate material for YouTube. After that, he got fired from Bizaardvark series.Jake Paul's boxing career began in August 2018 when he knocked out his fellow YouTuber Deji Olatunji in an amateur contest via TKO in the fifth round. Turning professional, Paul hit the YouTuber AnEsonGib in January 2020 via TKO in the first round. Here are Jake Paul's fights he has fought and how much he generated in those fights

Jake Paul and Ben Askren - $690,000

The fight occurred at the Mercedes-Benz Stadium in Atlanta, Georgia, on April 17, 2021. Ben is a former Olympic MMA artist. Jake won the battle through TKO in a minute and 59 seconds. Ben had to sign a special agreement that he won't be able to use any MMA tactics, or he would be fined $100000 for any move. This was the first fight where Jake fought with a pro-fighter.

Jake Paul and AnEsonGib - $1 million

AnEsonGib is a British YouTuber, and the match took place in 2020 at Banc of California Stadium. AnEsonGib, a veteran of numerous amateur and professional battles, could not knock down Jake. Jake delivered a tremendous right-hand blow to AnEsonGib, and the referee had to stop the fight. Jake was able to pocket $1 million for the bout.

Jake Paul and Tyron Woodley - $2 million

Jake was at it again with Woodley on August 2021 at Amalie Arena in Tampa, Florida. Jake, the problem child, was able to knock down Woodley. Jake delivered KO, which sent the former UFC welterweight champion to the ground. Jake was able to pocket -$2 million, Pay Per View revenue.

Jake Paul and Tommy Fury - $30million
