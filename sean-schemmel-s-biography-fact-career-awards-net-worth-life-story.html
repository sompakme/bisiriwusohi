Sean Schemmel is an American voice actor, ADR director and screenwriter.

Sean Schemmel is known for his works as a voice actor, particularly his work on the Dragon Ball series, voicing teen and adult Goku.

He previously worked as an ADR Director and Script Writer for NYAV Post, where he had directed and adapted into English several Japanese anime series. He also provided voices for a number of English versions of famous Japanese anime, television series and video games.
