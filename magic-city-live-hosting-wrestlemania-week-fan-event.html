Magic City Live Hosting WrestleMania Week Fan Event

Publish date: 2024-08-28

WrestleMania Week is always full of off-kilter wrestling adjacent activities for fans to attend. From showcases for alternate styles of sports entertainment to meet and greets and conventions, there’s something for everyone. There’s even something for EC3, who will bring his burgeoning YouTube show Magic City Live to Tampa.

https://twitter.com/MagicCityL1ve/status/1222635562848399363

On Thursday, April 2nd at The Orpheum, EC3, Drake Maverick, Percy Watson and Braun Strowman will host Magic City Mania. The show will feature the mid-season finale of their YouTube talk show alongside The Meat Karaoke Party. Magic City Live also promises special guests to be announced, but tickets are already on sale. You can attend the event for $25 or go all out on the $125 VIP option, which includes a special shirt, a lanyard, a signed picture of the cast, access to the balcony and a private bar, and the opportunity to compete for the Promo Bowl during the show.

If you haven’t yet seen EC3’s new venture, episodes premiere every Wednesday at 10 PM on their official YouTube channel.
