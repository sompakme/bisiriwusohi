Home Decor Gift Guide: TV Designers Pick Their Must-Haves

Publish date: 2024-07-05

A gift guide worth bookmarking! If you have a loved one who’s obsessed with home design TV shows, your search for the perfect holiday gift ends now. Some of the most notable names in interior design — including Chip and Joanna Gaines, Nate Berkus and Jeremiah Brent, Genevieve Gorder, Christina Anstead and Tarek El Moussa — spoke exclusively with Us Weekly ahead of the holidays, sharing what they’d get for the decorator in the family.

The list below features items of all price ranges — from an $18 art print that the former Fixer Upper hosts love, to an elaborate $688 set of handblown glasses that Berkus, 49, adores. His husband, 36, has quite a different take, reminding Us that everyone loves a simple cashmere sweater from J. Crew. The pair will next appear in season 2 of Rock the Block, airing on HGTV in 2021.
