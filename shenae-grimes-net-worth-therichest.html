Shenae Grimes has an estimated net worth of $4 million. She is a Canadian actress who began with a recurring role in 2004 by playing the lead character, Darcy Edwards, on the series Degrassi: The Next Generation.

In 2008, she left Canada to play the role of Annie Wilson in 90210, the Beverly Hills, 90210 spin-offs. Grimes also featured in Picture This and True Confessions of a Hollywood Starlet. In 2009, she was named by People magazine as one of the most beautiful people in the world, without makeup. Grimes also appeared in Our Lady Peace rock band music video titled "All You Did Was Save My Life" and a short fashion film Unzipped. In 2010, Grimes wrote and directed a video for her own song "Myself and I". She also appeared in horror film Scream 4 and Freddie Wong's YouTube video the "Gun Size Matters". Grimes was born in Toronto, in 1989 and is married to Josh Beech, a British model and singer.
