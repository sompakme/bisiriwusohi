Early Life and Career Beginnings

Growing up in Cleveland, Ohio, Drew Carey’s passion for comedy and performing began to develop at a young age. His love for making people laugh led him to pursue a career in the entertainment industry, and his journey towards success started with humble beginnings.

Carey’s early years were marked by hard work and determination. After graduating from James Ford Rhodes High School, he enlisted in the United States Marine Corps, serving for six years. It was during his time in the military that Carey discovered his natural talent for comedy, entertaining his fellow Marines with his quick wit and humor.

Following his honorable discharge from the Marines, Carey set his sights on a career in comedy. He began performing at local open mic nights, honing his skills and refining his comedic style. His hard work paid off when he caught the attention of television executives, leading to his first television appearance on “Star Search” in 1988.
