Report: Brogan Finlay, Jay Malachi Expected To Sign With WWE

Publish date: 2024-09-10

After participating in a tryout in September, Brogan Finlay, the son of Dave Finlay, is reportedly expected to sign with WWE.

As previously reported by Cassidy Haynes of Bodyslam.net, Brogan Finlay, Lucky Ali, and Jay Malachi were expected to report to the WWE Performance Center in January, as they had been offered deals following a tryout in September.

Per Mike Johnson of PWInsider, Brogan Finlay is expected to sign a developmental deal. He is at the WWE Performance Center in Orlando, Florida today.

Brogan has wrestled for various companies on the independent scene, including Game Changer Wrestling, ACTION, and Black Label Pro.

Dave, formerly known as Fit Finlay, is currently a producer with WWE. He is a former United States Champion.

Update: PWInsideralso reported that Jay Malachi is at the WWE Performance Center, and he is believed to have been signed” to an NXT deal. Malachi has wrestled for CZW, West Coast Pro, and he has competed on AEW Dark.

WrestleZone will provide more information as it becomes available.
