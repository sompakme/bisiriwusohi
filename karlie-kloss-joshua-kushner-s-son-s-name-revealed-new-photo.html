Karlie Kloss, Joshua Kushner's Son's Name Revealed: New Photo

Publish date: 2024-08-07

Too cute! Karlie Kloss shared a photo of her new baby with her husband, Joshua Kushner, and revealed the newborn’s name.

“Levi Joseph,” the model, 28, wrote via Instagram on Friday, April 16, captioning a picture of herself holding the baby’s hand while wearing a gold “Mama” ring. “3.11.21.”

Kloss and Kushner, 35, announced the arrival of Levi in March when the Oscar Health cofounder posted a photo of the baby to his Instagram account, but they did not share any further details about his name or birth date. Fellow model Emily Ratajkowski, who was also expecting at the time, left a comment on the photo reading, “Congrats Karlie.”

Several of the Kode With Klossy founder’s supermodel friends commented on the new photo, including Adriana Lima, Tyra Banks, Irina Shayk, Caroline Trentini and Carolyn Murphy. “Cutieeeeee I can’t wait to meet him,” wrote Lily Aldridge. “Baby!!!!!!!! Welcome to the world Levi! We love you!!!!!!” said celebrity stylist Karla Welch. Coco Rocha added, “Congrats mama!!! Sooo excited for this next chapters of your life.”

Her older sister, Kristine Kloss, also left a sweet message for her new nephew. “My favorite little guy, ever! Best snuggler and hand holder in the 🌎!” she wrote. “Love watching you grow into motherhood — you are such a natural, Kar!”

In March 2020, a source told Us Weekly that Karlie and her husband had “been trying to have a baby for a few months.” Six months later, Us confirmed that the model was expecting.

The Project Runway host wed Kushner in an intimate ceremony in October 2018 after they had dated for six years. “They wanted to make it very low-key,” an insider told Us after the nuptials. “They wanted to be as private as possible.”

The pair has always stayed tight-lipped about their relationship and seem to be following the same strategy when it comes to their son.
