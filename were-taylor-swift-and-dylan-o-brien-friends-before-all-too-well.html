Were Taylor Swift And Dylan O'Brien Friends Before 'All Too Well'?

Publish date: 2024-09-09

Taylor Swift has over the year become known as a celebrity with plenty of famous friends. While everyone knows of Swift's 'friends squad' that starred in her "Bad Blood" music video, she seems to have also made some new friends thanks to her music.

Today, we're taking a closer look at Taylor Swift's friendship with Dylan O'Brien. Have the two stars known each other before they worked together on the "All Too Well" music video? Keep on scrolling to find out!

Why Did Taylor Swift Pick Dylan O'Brien?

While some may think that Swift and O'Brien have known each other prior to "All Too Well" it turns out the musician was actually a fan of the Teen Wolf star.

"Dylan was my first choice for the 'All Too Well' short film because he has that versatility I was looking for," Taylor Swift told Bustle. "I’d seen his work and heard nothing but wonderful things about him as a person. Ultimately I want to work with people who love what they do and come at it with enthusiasm because that’s how I approach creating things too."

The musician also said she's a fan of the actor during her appearance on Late Night With Seth Meyers. "I watch everything he’s in. I’m a huge fan of his," the singer said. "They really just acted their a**es off," Swift added, referring to Dylan O'Brien's co-star from the music video, Sadie Sink.

While Swift was a fan of O'Brien for years, the actor also revealed that he loves T-Swift's music.

"Give any of the rest of us six months in quarantine and we look back on it and we’re like, 'S–t, I should have done more.' Give Taylor six months in quarantine, and she writes 50 hit songs," O'Brien told Coup De Main magazine in April 2021.

"I’m a big 'Mirrorball' fan," the actor added. I love 'My Tears Ricochet' and 'Mad Woman,' 'Epiphany' [and] 'The 1.' There’s so many more, but those are my highlights."

In fact, it seems as if Dylan O'Brien has been a Swiftie for a while. In an interview with The Cut, the actor was asked about his favorite Taylor Swift album.

"Oh, wow. I mean, I’m a big 1989 person, and I love Lover as well. 'Lover' is a go-to karaoke song for me. I can’t wait for her new album! I was bugging her the other day about when it was coming out," that actor said, referring to Swift's new studio album Midnights. "I guess I could say I’m in a Lover era. I’m just so obsessed with it."

Are Taylor Swift And Dylan O'Brien Friends Today?

During an appearance on The Tonight Show with Jimmy Fallon, Taylor Swift revealed that she frequently becomes close friends with the people she works with.

The musician admitted that she became "really close friends" with O'Brien after the two worked together on her music video for "All Too Well," and according to Swift, the two "hang out all the time" now.

"I've been very lucky to work with very nice, very talented people. And that's not something I ever take for granted," Swift told Fallon. "And a lot of the time, we end up really good friends."

During her appearance on The Tonight Show, Taylor Swift opened up about her friendship with Dylan O'Brien a bit more, and she even revealed that O'Brien played the drums on "Snow On The Beach," a song from Swift's new album Midnights.

"Dylan was just hanging out, drinking wine with us and listening to us," Swift reveals. "He wasn't serious, and so we were just sort of like, 'Oh, we haven't recorded the drums for this one yet...' And he just played the drums on the song. Sometimes it just happens like that."

For Entertainment Tonight, Dylan O'Brien revealed that he has a group chat with Taylor Swift and Sadie Sink. "We were just texting the other day. We have a little group chat, me, Taylor, and Sadie," Dylan said. "[Taylor is] just so genuine… She’s genuinely just someone who’s so involved and just proud of her work and is so grateful for any accomplishment or recognition for something that she just poured herself into. Talk about a special human."

It certainly seems as if Taylor Swift and Dylan O'Brien became close friends thanks to work — and hopefully fans get to see them collaborate again in the future!
