ROH TV Spoilers From Seattle (Taped On 9/30)

Publish date: 2024-09-22

On September 30, Ring of Honor held their latest set of TV tapings from the Climate Pledge Arena in Seattle, Washington. The matches will air on ROH TV on October 5.

The spoilers, courtesy of Fightful, are as follows:

  • : EJ Nduka defeated Jon Cruz
  • : Powerhouse Hobbs defeated Cody Chhun
  • Satoshi Kojima defeated Tony Nese
  • Josh Woods defeated Tyler Payne
  • : Athena (c) defeated “Legit” Leyla Hirsch
  • Rocky Romero defeated Christopher Daniels
  • Scorpio Sky defeated Fred Rosser
  • Willow Nightingale, Skye Blue & Kiera Hogan defeated Lady Frost & The Renegade Twins (Robyn Renegade & Charlette Renegade)
  • Ethan Page defeated Invictus Khash
  • TMDK (Shane Haste & Mikey Nicholls) defeated The Infantry (Shawn Dean & Carlie Bravo)
  • Darius Martin defeated Lee Johnson
  • Billie Starkz defeated Trish Adora
  • Shane Taylor Promotions (Shane Taylor & Lee Moriarty) defeated Iron Savages (Bronson & Boulder) (with Jacked Jameson) and Cole Karter & Griff Garrison (with Maria Kanellis-Bennett) and The WorkHorsemen (JD Drake & Anthony Henry)
  • Komander, Metalik & Gravity (with Alex Abrahantes) defeated Spanish Announce Project (Serpentico & Angelico) & Gringo Loco

WrestleZone has coverage of ROH TV as it airs every Thursday.
