Who Is Joshua Vacanti? Everything To Know About The Contestant From The Voice Season 21

Publish date: 2024-07-23

Joshua Vacanti is a Western New York singer who auditioned in The Voice season 21. Tune in to find out more about the contestant.

Joshua Vacanti performed the song ‘Into The Unknown’ by Panic! At The Disco in The Voice Blind Auditions.

After his performance, It didn’t take long for Ariana and John to take notice of his vivacious performance.

Despite the fact that both judges were anxious to have him join their side, it was John who was able to secure Joshua by instantly putting up a barrier against Ariana.

The Voice 2021: Joshua Vacanti Age

The age of Voice contestant Joshua Vacanti is 28 years old.

Speaking of his childhood, Joshua revealed that he had asthma and, was put on steroids.

Using steroids contributed to his bulkiness. Thus, Joshua was forced to endure a great deal of hardship.

But, Joshua was never the one who gave up easily, as while growing up, he completely changed his diet and maintained his weight.

He’s now eating a wide variety of vegetables. And he also intends to become a vegetarian in order to live a more contented and prosperous life.
