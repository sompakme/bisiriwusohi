Sandra Ababio Biography, Age, Career and Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-08-08

Sandra Sarfo Ababio is a Ghanaian actress, lawyer, TV host, model, film maker and producer. She gained popularity for featuring in both Ghana’s Kumawood movie and Nigeria’s Nollywood movies. Sandra Ababio was born on 18 May 1983 in Accra, Ghana to Naval Captain IK Ankobiah (Rtd) and Mrs Rita Ankobiah.

Educational Background

Sandra Ababio attended Tema Parents Association School and Services Basic School in Burma Camp, Accra. She obtained a degree in Law, from the University of Buckingham in 2005.

In 2009, Sandra Ababio obtained a Masters degree in International and Commercial Law, with a specialising in World Trade from University of Buckingham.

Sandra Ababio studied at Ghana School of Law from 2010 to 2012. She became a Ghanaian lawyer in 2013.

Career

Sandra Ababio started her career as a model in 2002. She contested for and won the Miss Ghana Pageant. Sandra Ababio represented Ghana at the Miss ECOWAS pageant and finished as the second runner up.

Sandra Ababio modeled for notable brands like Pistis, Ophelia Crossland, Kofi Ansah, Mina Evans, Wang Sambo, Mai Atafo, Vonne Couture and Duaba Serwa

Philanthropy

Sandra Ababio is a philanthropist. She founded The Legal Advocacy Foundation, a non government organization that educates ordinary Ghanaians about their legal rights.

Her foundation Sandra Ababio Foundation aims at eliminating drug abuse in Ghana while Fashion for Action aids the underprivileged through Haute couture fashion auctions.
