Dominik Mysterio Wins NXT North American Title On 7/18 WWE NXT

Publish date: 2024-08-11

The Judgment Day has captured more gold, as Dominik Mysterio has won his first singles title.

In recent weeks, The Judgment Day has made their presence known on WWE NXT. Damian Priest and Finn Balor defeated NXT Champion Carmelo Hayes and Trick Williams during the July 11 episode. During the show, “Dirty” Dominik Mysterio challenged NXT North American Champion Wes Lee to face him in a title match.

The two stars headlined the July 18 episode, as Lee put his title on the line against “Dirty” Dom. In the closing moments, Balor got on the apron, Priest tried to interfere, and Rhea Ripley smashed Lee with her Women’s World Championship. Mysterio then pinned Lee for the win.

Lee previously won the title at NXT Halloween Havoc in October 2022.
