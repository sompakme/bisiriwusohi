Christina Aguilera's Halloween 'Fun House' Is A Nightmare Come True

Publish date: 2024-09-03

Halloween is one of the best times of the year for adults and children alike. It's the perfect opportunity to let out our inner child, get dressed up in a fun, or spooky costume, play with some face makeup, and get out for a wild and scary night!

Seeing the sights of the neighborhood houses dressed up in their best Halloween decor is always a treat, and many people go all out when it comes to decorating. It has just been revealed that Christina Aguilera is the one to beat when it comes to Halloween houses.

She recently let the crew from Cribs into her home, and the cameras captured what can only be described as the most elaborate, incredibly detailed, fully decorated nightmare that we have ever seen.

You need to see this to believe it.... Grab your best costume and tune in to this awesome adventure!

An Aguilera Halloween

Christina Aguilera definitely knows how to have a lot of Halloween fun. She has spookily transformed her enormous mansion into a Halloween fun house that is so jam packed with decorations that we don't know where to begin in describing them all. Her recent Instagram video reveals frightening decorations at every turn. Her front entrance, the inner archway in her foyer... her entire massive backyard, and everything in between is fully decorated.

She has included some enormous statement pieces that steal the show. Enormous, over-sized monsters and many types of various spooky skeletons can be found at every turn. She has included some Christmas lights strewn across her yard, and she even got into a decorated golf cart to zip around the property. She has decor...everywhere.

This is the best Halloween look we've ever seen!  Her two children, Max and Summer Rain must be blissfully happy living in a haunted house that is decorated to this degree!
