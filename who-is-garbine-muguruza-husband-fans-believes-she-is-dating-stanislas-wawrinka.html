Who Is Garbine Muguruza Husband? Fans Believes She Is Dating Stanislas Wawrinka

Publish date: 2024-08-02

Garbine Muguruza is a Spanish tennis player who does not have a husband while her fans Believe she is dating Stanislas Wawrinka. Explore the details about her in the article below.  

Garbine Muguruza is a professional tennis player who has been a flag bearer for the Spanish team on the WTA tour for many years now.

Muguruza has a career-high ranking of world No. 1 in singles and a highest WTA doubles ranking of 10.

Moreover, the athlete has won nine singles titles, including two majors: the 2016 French Open and the 2017 Wimbledon Championships.

Who Is Garbine Muguruza Husband?

Garbine Muguruza is known to be single and unmarried thus she does not have a husband or even a boyfriend for now.

The professional tennis player has had many years of experience in tennis, while, she is yet to feel the love and care of a partner as of now. 

Based on her available information, she has not been in a romantic relationship with anyone as of now.

Moreover, the 28 years old might be more focused on her career than her marital status. Thus, she is investing her full-time in her games and matches.

Is Garbine Muguruza Dating Stanislas Wawrinka? 

Many fans believe that professional tennis player Garbine Muguruza is dating Stanislas Wawrinka.

Wawrinka is a fellow Swiss professional tennis player; he made a post with Muguruza on his Instagram with the caption ‘Hot and humid with Mugu’ and added some juicy emoji in June of 2020.

Moreover, they also uploaded posts in the pool separately, which seems to be the same pool. These things convinced their fan to speculate they are a couple.

However, it doesn’t seem the case as Garbine Muguruza has revealed that she is currently single and not dating anyone. Moreover, Garbine has chosen to focus on tennis for the time being.

Garbine Muguruza Family Details

Garbine Muguruza has a family of five, including her parents and her siblings.

The athlete was born to her Venezuelan mother, Scarlet Blanco, and her Spanish father, José Antonio Muguruza, who hails from Azkoitia in Gipuzkoa, Basque Country.

Moreover, the professional tennis player has two elder brothers named Asier and Igor, and she has dual Spanish-Venezuelan citizenship.

Furthermore, Muguruza moved to Spain with her family when she was six, and she began training at the Bruguera Tennis Academy near Barcelona.

Garbine Muguruza Net Worth Revealed

Garbine Muguruza has an estimated net worth of $12 million as of 2021.

The professional tennis player accumulated $6.6 million, of which $4.5 came through her wide range of endorsements.

Furthermore, she was ranked sixth in Forbes’ list of highest-paid female athletes in 2020. Moreover, she is also an advocate for peace and refugee habilitation.

Furthermore, many bands, including Beats, Adidas, Babolat, Rolex, Maui Jim, and Cesar Insurance, have sponsored Muguruza. And the athletes’ apparel deal with Adidas is considered one of the most lucrative ones in tennis.
