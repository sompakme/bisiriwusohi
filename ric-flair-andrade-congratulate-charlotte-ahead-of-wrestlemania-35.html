Ric Flair &amp; Andrade Congratulate Charlotte Ahead Of WrestleMania 35

Publish date: 2024-09-22

Charlotte Flair was infamously referred to as “Ric Flair’s daughter” by ESPN, and Ric says that changes today. “It’s official, Charlotte is no longer Ric Flair’s daughter,” he wrote. “I am Charlotte Flair’s dad! All the accolades that have come my way over time are diminished by her accomplishments now! I am so proud of you Charlotte.”

He later added, “Today, history will be made and The Queen will take her rightful place with Becky Lynch and Ronda Rousey as the first women to ever main event WrestleMania!”

Equally as supportive is Charlotte’s boyfriend, SmackDown star Andrade. He said that tonight was her night and called her “my beautiful lady.”
