Sean X-Pac Waltman Says Chyna Confronted Triple H at Roddy Pipers Funeral, Says Security Al

Publish date: 2024-07-18

According to The Wrestling Observer, Triple H and Chyna were both in attendance for Roddy Piper’s funeral in Portland, Oregon on Tuesday.

The report notes Triple H and Chyna were in the same room for about an hour, but they never spoke to each other.

A fan took to Twitter and asked Sean “X-Pac” Waltman, who was also present at the funeral, the following question:

“chyna at roddy pipers funeral? Did she ever even work with him? Reeks of opportunism to be around hhh.”

Waltman responded by saying the following:

She may have met him a few times & you’re exactly right.She bum rushed Hunter & said ‘Sorry for Everthing!” Pathetic https://t.co/EySVX5GVnw

— Sean Waltman (@TheRealXPac) August 13, 2015

Waltman then Tweeted, but then deleted, the following:

“I wasnt going to mention Chyna crashing Roddy’s funeral, but I guess ppl already know. ‘Sorry for everything’? I thought she was the victim.”

Waltman then offered the following:

I was informed by a reliable source that she didnt “crash” the funeral. She was apparently invited. I have to correct myself.

— Sean Waltman (@TheRealXPac) August 13, 2015

I apologize for using the term “Crash”.

— Sean Waltman (@TheRealXPac) August 13, 2015

She was very close to being taken down by security, whether she knows it or not, after the threats & lies she’s told about him.

— Sean Waltman (@TheRealXPac) August 13, 2015
