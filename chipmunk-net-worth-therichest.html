Chipmunk (Jahmaal Noel Fyffe) is an English hip-hop rapper who has an estimated net worth of $3.5 million. Chipmunk has accumulated the net worth from his record contract with renowned record label Columbia Records (part of Sony Music UK) and his songwriting work.

The 23 year old rapper was born in Tottenham, England and began his music career in 2008 after signing a contract with Columbia Records. The following year he released his debut single “Chip Diddy Chip” and followed it up with hit singles like “Diamond Rings” and “Oopsy Daisy”, his most successful single to date.

Chipmunk released his debut album “I Am Chipmunk” in 2009 and 2 years later released the second one titled “Transition “.He has won 2 MOBO Awards for the Best UK Newcomer and Best Hip-Hop Act as well as The UGG Award ( Urban / Garage / Grime) to date and nominated for several other awards . Chipmunk has since shortened his name to “Chip” and joined Grandhustle Records owned by fellow rapper T.I.
