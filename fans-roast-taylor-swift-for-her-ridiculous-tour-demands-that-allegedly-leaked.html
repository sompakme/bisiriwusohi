Who Pays For Taylor Swift's Tour Rider?

According to Parade, tour riders are a common practice for just about every single type of artist, regardless of genre. The requests artists make of a specific venue may include things like safety requirements, allergies and dietary restrictions, or contain a list of demands for their dressing room. On occasion, these demands can stretch into ridiculous territories.

Who pays for the tour rider requests can vary on the situation. If the artist is playing at a festival, the promoters will cover the costs. In the case of a big concert like Taylor Swift's, the concert promoter will tend to pay the bill. Typically, there's a negotiation surrounding the contents of the rider.
