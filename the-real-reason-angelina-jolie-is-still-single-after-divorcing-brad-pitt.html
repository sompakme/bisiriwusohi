Is Brad Pitt Single?

Brad Pitt is a fine man who seems to be better as he ages. However, after his separation from Angelina Jolie, he struggled in getting back into the dating game after their 12-year relationship. Despite his dating slump, fans still linked him with different women—past and new—over the past few years. Jennifer Aniston, Brad's ex-wife, and Andra Day, Stevie Wonder's singing protégé, were involved in the rumors, but both denied allegations of their rumored relationship with Brad Pitt, supporting Brad Pitt's claim of his single status.

Angelina Jolie admits she is single because of her high standards in looking for a future partner. Are Angelina Jolie And Brad Pitt Reconciling?

The two did not end on good terms after Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie's public break-up. However, if co-parenting and rebuilding their friendship will be better for their kids, they can reconcile. We'll always be a family." After an eventful divorce process full of declarations of conflicts between the two, fans believe they are in the works of slowly finding a middle ground for the kids.

Why Is Brad Pitt Suing Angelina Jolie?

Brad Pitt and Angelina own a French estate named Château Miraval, where they got married. After their separation, Angelina allegedly sold her share of the property without Brad Pitt's consent which upset the Fight Club star. This decision upset Brad because of its sentimental value and uninformed sale to a Russian oligarch.

In Brad Pitt's lawsuit, he says Angelina's action caused "gratuitous harm" to him. But Angelina responds by saying that selling her vineyard share was a "painful decision, with a heavy heart." Only when fans thought Brangelina was finally starting to mend their broken relationship with their children the new legal conflict shows it is still slow progress.

Angelina Jolie getting sued by her ex-husband also gives another reason for the Hollywood actress to focus on her children's healing despite the feud with her divorcee. As shown in their ongoing lawsuit, the formerly golden couple's lack of communication somehow proves that being single would be the best thing for their kids right now.
