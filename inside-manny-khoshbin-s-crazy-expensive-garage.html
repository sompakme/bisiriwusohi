Manny Khoshbin may be a celebrity real estate mogul. Still, he is also known for his stunning car collection, which features some of America's most exclusive and rare customized cars.

Manny Khoshbin was only 14 years old when his family moved to America, where he received his real estate license in 1992. After establishing himself as a successful realtor, he began his firm, The Khoshbin Company, which has sold properties of over 2.5 million square feet. His vast enterprise has expanded to various ventures, and Khoshbin, who was once homeless, has grown a fortune of $250 million. Active social media personality makes fans aware that the real estate mogul is a car fanatic. Not only does Khoshbin love to collect cars, but he is also well aware of the technology behind every vehicle he owns.

From supercars customized by Hermès to buying special edition vehicles that travel at lightning speed, Manny Khoshbin has each supercar in multiple figures. A true car enthusiast, let’s look at some of the most expensive cars in his collection.

Bugatti Chiron Hermès Edition ($6 million)

One of the most exclusive cars in America, Manny Khoshbin, collaborated with the French fashion house Hermès to create a one-of-a-kind Bugatti Chiron Veyron. According to Car and Driver, the Bugatti is splashed with Craie off-white paint by Hermès and comes with a custom cashmere fabric at the door panels. The cream-colored interior has the badge of Dressed By Hermès printed on the center stack.

Bugatti Veyron Grand Sport ($3 million)

Manny Khoshbin has purchased every Bugatti he has loved over the years. In 2006, he was one of the first to buy the Veyron Grand Sport dipped in blue and white colorways. The car comes up with a power-packed 8.0-liter W16 engine with 987 HP. Mansory tuned his Veyron Grand Sport, and Khoshbin also owns the Veyron Grand Sport Vitesse Rembrandt Edition.

Porsche 911 GT3 ($161,000)

One of his favorite purchases to date, purchased in 2022 Porsche 911 GT3, in the signature shade of canary yellow. Khoshbin is often spotted driving the car down canyon roads or posting videos of its speed and design on social media. Based on the latest generation of 911, he believes that it is the best car money can buy.

Pagani Huayra Hermès Edition ($5 million)

In another collaboration with Hermès, Khoshbin contacted Pagani to create a car designed by his favorite designer, and the automaker complied. The dark brown vehicle was given a refreshing interior and stocked with a V-12 twin-turbo engine sourced by AMG, as noted by Motor 1. Each interior item was custom-made in Paris by Hermès.

Rolls Royce Phantom Drophead ($445,000)

The Phantom is a unique white-on-white car from Rolls Royce in the collection. In the family car, Khoshbin is often photographed in the car with his wife and children. The Phantom Drophead has only clocked 5,400 miles. It comes with a V-12 engine and eight-speed automatic transmission that prances to a top speed of 155 MPH.

Rolls Royce Dawn ($356,500)

Another Rolls Royce in the midst, the Dawn, is a stunning convertible in Manny’s collection, often used by his family. The lovely British car is one of the five owned by him. White, the mogul, prefers to drive around in his McLaren and Bugatti supercars. The Dawn is often spotted in the background while working out next to his fleet of vehicles.

Ford GT Heritage Edition ($500,000)

After owning a string of Bugatti and Mercedes supercars, Khoshbin added a stunning Ford GT to his collection, all the way from the Heritage Edition. The mogul’s favorite setting is the unique V-Max mode exclusive to the edition, as reported by Auto Evolution. He has claimed that the Ken Miles tribute edition is the best-sounding V6-powered car in his garage.

Mercedes SLR AMG McLaren ($740,000)

Owning not one or two but eight whopping Mercedes SLR AMG McLaren, Manny Khoshbin has always been a fan of the German-British hybrid. His latest purchase occurred in 2021 when he bought two SLR AMG McLaren models simultaneously: one with a silver exterior and red interior and the other with an all-black interior and exterior.

Ferrari Daytona SP3 ($2.2 million)

When Ferrari announced its Daytona SP3 model, gearheads worldwide went crazy to get their hands on the supercar, and Manny Khoshbin wasn’t far behind. While he once had a fallout with Ferrari due to a dispute, he was willing to start a new relationship with the automaker through the 828 HP-powered Daytona SP3. The mogul has previously owned a Ferrari 360 Spider and F40.

Custom Carbon Black McLaren P1 ($1.1 million)

While all McLaren P1s are made from carbon fiber, it is only limited to the body panels. In 2014, McLaren released 20 limited edition hypercars with carbon fiber everywhere. As mentioned by Yahoo, when Khoshbin first purchased the car in 2012, McLaren declined to offer customization. However, once the full-body carbon package was announced, he became one of the 20 lucky people to get the car.

Other notable cars in Manny Khoshbin’s garage include the Tesla Model X, Porsche 935, Rolls Royce Ghost, McLaren Speedtail, McLaren Senna, and many more. Manny Khoshbin will continue to flourish his collection with more customized stunning supercars as a car enthusiast with a sprawling net worth.
