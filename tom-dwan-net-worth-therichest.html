Tom Dwan is an American Poker player whose worth is $10 million. Born on July 30th, 1986, he was part of Team Full Tilt and in most times, played online Poker. He resigned as the brand ambassador of the team in 2012. Tom Dwan has also attained great success in live tournaments and featured in many Poker shows including High Stakes Poker and Poker After Dark.

He has won money in many live poker tournaments and has featured on NBC's National Heads-Up Poker Championship in the fourth to the seventh seasons of Poker After Dark. He studied at Boston University before he dropped to pursue full-time Poker. He was just nineteen years when he won money in his first live tournament, by finishing twelfth and won the next cash at the age of twenty-one at world poker tour in 2007. As of 2013, his overall live tournaments is estimated to exceed $ 2.2 million.
