Liv Morgan Attends Packers vs. Chiefs, Takes Picture With Simone Biles

Publish date: 2024-08-05

Liv Morgan took in the action at Lambeau Field on Sunday night.

On December 3, the Green Bay Packers faced the Kansas City Chiefs at Lambeau Field on Sunday Night Football. The game aired on NBC and Peacock, and WWE, whose Premium Live Events air on Peacock, shared a clip of Liv Morgan attending the game. In the video, Morgan was shown with Packers running back Aaron Jones and Olympic gymnast Simone Biles.

WWE also shared pictures in which Morgan was shown with Biles and actor Tony Shaloub, the former star of Monk.

Liv Morgan last wrestled on the July 17 episode of WWE RAW. There, she and Raquel Rodriguez lost the WWE Women’s Tag Team Championship to Chelsea Green and Sonya Deville. In September, Morgan confirmed that she was out with an injury. Rodriguez commented on Morgan’s status in October and noted that she assumed Morgan would return sooner rather than later.

As of this writing, there is no word on when Liv Morgan will return to the ring. WrestleZone will provide more information as it becomes available.
