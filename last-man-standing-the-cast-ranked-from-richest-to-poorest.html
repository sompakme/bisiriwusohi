9 Tim Allen As Mike Baxter On Last Man Standing Has A Net Worth Of $100 Million

Tim Allen played the lead role of Mike Baxter in the show, who is the patriarch of the family and a marketing director for a sporting goods store. Tim Allen's big break was on the TV show Home Improvement (1991–1999). The royalties from the reruns of Home Improvement alone have substantially added to Allen's net worth. It is a classic sitcom. They once spoke of a reboot, but that was in 2020, and a lot of things fell apart in 2020.

During his time on Home Improvement, Allen also got into film. He starred in movies like The Santa Clause (1994) and Galaxy Quest (1999). Tim Allen then provided the voice for Buzz Lightyear in Pixar's Toy Story series, starting in 1995. The franchise became a major success, and his character is still popular today.

Continuing on with his success, Tim Allen appeared in various other films and television shows, including Wild Hogs,Christmas with the Kranks, The Shaggy Dog, and guest roles in series like The Office.

Amidst all of his acting roles, Tim Allen has also had his share of legal battles and personal issues that nearly got him put in prison for a long time. There are no financial records laying out what this ordeal cost Allen, but as of today, Tim Allen's net worth is still a massive $100 million, so he isn't doing too badly.
