Meagan Good Debunks BBL Rumors, Shows Off &quot;Gym Booty&quot; In Bikini Photos

Publish date: 2024-07-31

Meagan Good has corrected those who think her toned body is anything more than a result of the gym.

On Monday (Nov. 13), the beloved actress posted a montage video of beach photos and clips to Instagram. In the post, Good shows off her golden toned body, and even gives glimpses of her derrière in a tiny yellow bikini.

Although the Harlem star is all-smiles on the sandy beachfront, that didn’t stop social media from questioning if she had a BBL.

“The new BBL looking [fire],” one user commented, with Good responding, “Nah homie. That’s a 42-year-old petite, ‘I worked for that’ gym booty.”

See below.

This wouldn’t be the first time Good has been accused of altering her appearance.

In March, the 42-year-old covered ESSENCE magazine, where she opened up about the public previously accusing her of bleaching her skin.

“I was like, I look so crazy I just have to sit in this, and let people think what they want to think, and there’s nothing I can do about it,” she expressed about sun-damage products that severely lightened her overall skin tone in 2020.

She added, “I didn’t realize it at the time, but my concern with what other people thought about me was such a thing that I had to go through something like that where I literally had no other option, and I had to find my joy and my peace regardless of what everyone was thinking.”

Hopeful that people can fully understand who she is one day, she told the publication, “I think people are starting to get who I am. I think for a long time they didn’t. And I had to get to a place where I stopped caring in order for me to walk into the place where people now are like, ‘No, I actually get her.’”
