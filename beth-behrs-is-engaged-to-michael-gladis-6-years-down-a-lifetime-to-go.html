Beth Behrs Is Engaged to Michael Gladis: '6 Years Down a Lifetime to Go'

Publish date: 2024-09-28

She said yes! Beth Behrs is one happy girl after a sweet proposal on an NYC rooftop on Sunday, July 10.

After a full day playing tourist in the Big Apple, the 2 Broke Girls star, 30, and Mad Men actor Michael Gladis headed up to the rooftop of the Parkside Lounge NYC, where Gladis, 38, popped the question.

“💍💍💍💍💕💕💕6 years down a lifetime to go! #love,” Behrs wrote via Instagram on Monday, July 11. 

Fittingly, Behrs wore a white button-down sundress on the day of her proposal, pairing it with a navy hat and matching navy booties with star details.

Immediately after the proposal, the couple’s friends and family joined in for an engagement party featuring live music and a photo booth. Behrs and her fiancé showed off her newly minted bling in a series of lively photos that highlight their excitement. “Can’t wait to celebrate! 💍💍💍#love,” the Hello My Name is Doris actress wrote while thanking guests in a follow-up post. 

Ahead of the big question, the couple spent a romantic day near Central Park, enjoying a boat ride underneath the iconic Bow Bridge and touring the Natural History Museum in the Upper West Side neighborhood of Manhattan. 

“Date day At the museum. Happy #Sunday,” she wrote, followed by a selfie with well-placed antlers belonging to a creature inside. “New accessory,” Behrs captioned the joking image.
