Here's Why Lisa Rinna's Lips Look Like That

Publish date: 2024-09-05

Highlights

  • Lisa Rinna popularized lip injections before Kylie Jenner's billion-dollar beauty business.
  • Lisa Rinna endured difficulty in restoring her lips with scar tissue but rectified it with surgery.
  • "Queen of big lips" Rinna started Rinna Beauty makeup, tapping into her daughters' collaboration.

Lisa Rinna is best known for being a cast member of the popular Bravo reality series The Real Housewives. After joining the ladies of Beverly Hills in Season Five, Lisa Rinna and her lips have constantly been the talk of the town!

While her explosive personality, unfiltered sense of humor, and iconic hairstyle speak for themselves, Lisa Rinna's lips are all anyone could talk about - that and her marriage to Harry Hamlin!

The star herself knows that her lips are always the center of attention, and she isn't shy about admitting that one single bit. You could say she is "owning it, baby!"

The actress has been sporting her luscious lips since the 90s. However, with enhanced lips being a thing of today, fans are curious as to how Lisa Rinna's lips were so on-trend and how she managed to get them to look like that!

Lisa Rinna Got Her Lips Injected

Lisa Rinna got her lips injected when she was 24 years old

It's become quite the trend to enhance one's lips, so much so that Kylie Jenner managed to create a billion-dollar beauty company after it. While Kylie may have popularized lip injections, Lisa Rinna's lips were showing off the results of Restylane WAY before Kylie was!

Lisa has always been pretty vocal about her lip enhancements, and she certainly wasn't shy about it when she was on RHOBH. It was not too much of a shock when she was asked about her lips on The Today Show with Hoda.

Lisa Rinna explained that the whole craze of getting her lips transformed started when Lisa Rinna saw Barbara Hershey’s film Beaches in the year 1988. Barbara’s full pout attracted her so much that she immediately decided to get her lips plumped with one of her best girlfriends, labeling it as a "stupid thing".

Then, at the age of 24, she signed up for permanent lip plumper without giving it a second thought. Lisa got four injections to plant permanent silicone fillers into her top lip, all while leaving the lower lip alone!

Lisa Rinna Faced Difficulty With Her Augmented Lip

Lisa Rinna had scar tissue built up in her lips, requiring assistance from a plastic surgeon to fix

Considering Lisa Rinna got permanent lip fillers, it was challenging for her to get back to her original look, which is why she sported the big lips for so long!

Despite her iconic look, Lisa wanted them gone, so much that she attempted to take cortisone shots to decrease lip swelling. However, she wasn't successful.

Lisa attempted to take cortisone shots to decrease lip swelling, but it didn’t help at all. Her lip started looking more swollen and worse. Soon she started getting negative comments about her lips from the audience and media.

Luckily for Lisa, she managed to get her lips restored by Beverly Hills plastic surgeon, Garth Fisher. Lisa Rinna's lips not only remained as iconic as they always were, but she now knows how to keep them modest, yet fabulous.

Like Mother, Like Daughters

Lisa Rinna's daughters, Delilah Belle and Amelia Gray have followed in her footsteps and have collaborated with Rinna Beauty

While being the queen of "big hair and big lips" as Lisa Rinna puts it, the RHOBH star is also balancing being a mom to supermodels, Delilah Belle, and Amelia Gray.

The duo has also followed in mom's footsteps by enhancing their pout slightly too, however, considering their faces are their money makers, they didn't play around with it as much as Rinna did back in the day.

Delilah Belle and Amelia Gray have their lips plastered across magazine covers, making appearances in fashion shows and spreads and collaborating with Rinna on her lip colors for Rinna Beauty.
