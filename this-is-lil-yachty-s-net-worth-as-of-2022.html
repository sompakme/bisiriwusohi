Miles Parks McCollum is known to his fans as Lil Yachty, and he has been credited as being one of the biggest success stories to come of the rap world. He has been mesmerizing fans with his music and his larger than life persona since he first emerged on the music scene in 2015, and continues to grow and expand his fan base with the passing of each day. His contributions to the rap game have been recognized by many other famous artists, and he is in high demand when it comes to big-name collaborations. Lil Yachty lives a life of luxury and isn't afraid of enjoying the millions of dollars that he is earning. He is just 24 years old, and he has already amassed an $8 million fortune.

Making $8 Million In Less Than 7 Years

As a young millionaire, Lil Yachty knows first-hand that hard work and dedication to his craft have already paid off. He has passionately thrown himself into creating music that reflects his views and life experiences. He is currently signed to Quality Control Music, and is fully reaping the benefits of his privileged lifestyle, while he continues to plug away at creating more hits.

Originally from Mableton, Lil Yachty first attracted the attention of fans when he emerged on the music scene in 2015 with hit singles such as Minnesota, and One Night. It wasn't until 2016 that his first mix tape made its rounds in in the industry, and it immediately generated interest from a variety of producers and rappers that were interested in connecting with Lil Yachty on a professional level.

He started off humbly living with his friend when he moved from Atlanta to New York in pursuit of his music dreams, and now, Lil Yachty is credit for earning his $8 million fortune in less than 7 years.

Big Music Moves

Lil Yachty made some big moves to get to where he is today. He started off networking with fashion personalities and went on to establish an impressive fan following on Instagram. He instantly recognized the power of social media and put his focus on connecting with his fans and followers on social media, and using that platform to further elevate his fame.

Those steps led to his collaboration with Burberry Perry, a leading producers that would soon collaborate with Lil Yachty to elevate his image and expand on the exposure of his music to the masses.

The continuation of his big music moves weaved together Burberry, Lil Yachty, and a new collaborator by the name of K$upreme, which soon became known as ‘The Saling Team’. Lil Yachty then catapulted to instant fame and new heights of success when Drake’s OVO Sound Radio aired his previously released single, Minnesota which soon saw an immense amount of fan attention.

He then released his track 1Night, which immediately went viral and officially put the spotlight on Lil Yachty as being a bonified star. He has since gone on to musically collaborate with the likes of Kanye West, among other influential heavyweights that recognize Lil Yachty's talents.

Big Income, Big Bills

The rise to fame was a fairly quick one for Lil Yachty, and as quickly as he began to infuse his bank account with millions of dollars, he began spending his fortune frivolously.  He has publicly declared that he sees no real benefit from stockpiling money in a bank account, and states that he would rather enjoy his money while he is still living.

He has become known as being a heavy-spender and is always adorned in expensive, oversized jewelry as he dines in the most expensive, exclusive restaurants everywhere he goes. As he embarks on his shopping sprees, Lil Yachty often snaps photos and posts his big purchases on social media.

Perhaps the most shocking revelation pertaining to his finances, is the enormity of his monthly bills. Lil Yachty has declared his routine monthly expenditures as being in the range of $52,000. He says; “Every month, I got $52,000 in bills. Monthly, you know what I’m saying? And, I have to work … Let’s say I got $13 million in the bank, right? If my overhead is $52,000 every month ’cause of my houses, and my staff and cars and insurance and all these things I take care of and all these people, that $13 [million] will run down. Because that’s just the bills. I still like to live. I’m still a rapper. I still wanna go to nice dinners, fly my girl out, go to see my girl. We still wanna do all these things.”

Diversifying His Income Streams

Lil Yachty has a solid awareness of his huge expenses, and recognizes that he needs to continue earning a substantial amount of money if he wants to maintain the lifestyle he has grown accustomed to.  Proving to have financial foresight, he has declared his awareness of the benefits of diversifying his income stream.

In addition to the multi-millions he earns by producing his music, Lil Yachy has put his focus on earning money while he sleeps. “You have to be making money while you’re sleeping. Never just have one source of income. Even if it’s six figures. Ni**as could be making $200[,000] a show, six shows a week, but that still should not be the only bag,” he says. Lil Yachty went on to state; "It’s so many things you could do to make that money double, triple while you sleep, while you snoring, while you working for the other job.”
