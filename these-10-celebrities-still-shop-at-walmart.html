These 10 Celebrities Still Shop At Walmart

Publish date: 2024-07-22

Imagine a celebrity buying a pair of socks at Walmart before boarding that private jet to fly to a fashion show in Milan. Many celebrities and public figures still value a good deal despite having large salaries and great net worth. Nicolas Cage, Justine Bieber, and Jessica Alba are just a few celebrities who have been spotted shopping at budget stores. Big-box stores provide a vast selection of goods, affordable rates, and handy locations. So perhaps it's not surprising that shoppers from all walks of life frequent the stores, regardless of how much money they make or have in their bank accounts. All ten of these celebrities have shopped at Walmart.

10 Beyoncé

Just when we thought Beyoncé had given her fans everything as an early Christmas gift in 2020, the singer paid a surprise visit to a Walmart in Massachusetts and drove them into a frenzy once more. The XO singer stopped into the Tewksbury, Massachusetts Walmart at around 7 o'clock to pick up her new album, buy a present for her daughter Blue Ivy, and give a treat to every fan in the store. After moving through the aisles and wheeling her own cart, Beyoncé welcomed a happy salesman at the register who was giggling uncontrollably.

9 Justin Bieber

In 2014, Justin Bieber was observed at a Walmart in Canada, where he was not exactly acting himself. The Daily Mail stated that a fellow customer posted a Facebook tirade stating that Bieber had been opening food products without paying for them and hurling objects about the aisles. Additionally, because of his behavior, the singer of What Do You Mean is producing problems and getting viral for the event.The overwhelming majority of his fans are delighted for his appearances in shopping centers like Walmart.

8 Miley Cyrus

Miley Cyrus and her ex-husbandLiam Hemsworth were reportedly seen shopping at a Walmart in Cullman, Alabama, according to The Cullman Times. She has, however, been connected to the establishment in other ways as well. She even saved a puppy that had been abandoned in a Walmart parking lot. She even had a clothing line with the business. Miley explained how she came up with the $2000 Walmart purchase during one of her interviews for Jeremy Scott's New York Fashion Week show. She remarked that it is actually very simple because she finds many great discounts online.

7 Arnold Schwarzenegger

The actor tweeted about his Walmart shopping in 2015. He claimed to have gone to Walmart in Arkansas to check on the Terminator exhibitions and replenish on camouflage robes and pajamas. The next year, in front of a cheering crowd of shoppers, Arnold Schwarzenegger spent $300 at a Walmart in New Mexico, according to a store employee. Schwarzenegger went shopping for jackets and long sleeve shirts for the actors and crew of his upcoming film, which was being shot nearby. TMZ claims that Schwarzenegger's security staff helped by keeping would-be autograph seekers from getting too near.

6 Dakota Fanning

Actress Dakota Fanning is another well-known person who has indulged in some Walmart retail therapy over the years. The ex-girlfriend of Henry Fry was seen in a Baton Rouge location while she was filming Breaking Dawn and was gracious enough to take some time out to pose for photos with excited fans. Dakota Fanning has frequently been seen shopping at Walmart for her favorite foods and outfits while having fun.

5 Rihanna

In the Canadian town of Kamloops, Walmart was where Rihanna was reportedly spotted in 2013, according to The Mirror. With sunglasses, a baseball cap, and headphones on, the singer was seen loading up on beverages and attempting to blend in while doing so. As a successful businesswoman, Rihanna offers a line of products on Walmart's website, including fragrances.

4 Chris Brown

In 2009, Chris Brown who had assault charges was startled to learn that the Walmart in Wallingford, Connecticut, did not carry any copies of his most recent album, Graffiti. The musician lashed out on Twitter, and Walmart retaliated with a statement claiming that Brown's allegations were false. The CD is available at every Walmart location across the country, including the one in Wallingford mentioned in the article, claims the retail store.

3 Amy Roloff

Amy Roloff, the star of Little People, Big World, was once discovered by a fan at a nearby Walmart and was gracious enough to pose for a photo with him. The local newspaper then published the image. This demonstrates that spotting a celebrity at Walmart is not all that common. Roloff's divorce from her first spouse, Matt Roloff, was finalized in May 2016. Roloff filed for divorce. When asked if she would consider dating again soon after the breakup, she said that she believed someone would be truly interested and looking forward to doing typical things like shopping.

2 Britney Spears

Mother-of-two Britney Spears is well known for her affinity for Walmart shopping. Many of her followers adore how authentic she remains on a daily basis. In Walmart, she is having a blast. The singer who lashed out on the documentaries about her is seen blatantly coveting a plaid top in black and white. She looks fantastic in everything she wears, and it's nice to see a well-known mom shopping on a budget rather than shelling out $1,000 for a blouse that might be covered in mud, food, spit-up, or all of the above.

1 Holly Madison

Holly Madison, a different famous mother, enjoys shopping at Walmart. She is the mother of two adorable kids, a son named Forest and a daughter named Rainbow. She is well known for her time as Hugh Hefner's primary girlfriend and for appearing in the reality television series The Girls Next Door, which followed her time living in the Playboy mansion. She has a motivation for clinging to her celebrity despite wanting to lead a normal life. Even though Madison is well-known, she frequently shops at Walmart, and people adore her for it.
