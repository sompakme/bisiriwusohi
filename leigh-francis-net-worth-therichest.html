An English entertainer currently married to Jill Carter, Leigh Francis’s major TV break came in 2002 when he was offered £250,000 to release a TV series titled Bo' Selecta!. The show primarily features Leigh Francis who impersonates different celebrities by wearing face masks. The TV series aired from 2002 to 2006 which lasted for 5 series.

When the show ended, Leigh Francis created another successful program titled Keith Lemon's Very Brilliant World Tour. Subsequently, he created Celebrity Juicewhich was aired for 8 seasons.

Leigh Francis has also ventured into the world of hosting. He hosted programs such as Sing If You Can and Keith Lemon's LemonAid. In 2012, Leigh Francis started shooting for his debut film titled  Keith Lemon: The Film.
