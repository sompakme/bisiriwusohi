Who is American Voice Actor Shawn Fonteno?

Shawn Fonteno, or OG Solo, is an American rapper, voice actor, actor, and recording artist who voiced and acted as Franklin Clinton in GTA V and an unknown Grove Street Families member Fam1 in GTA: San Andreas.

As an actor, Shawn Fonteno has appeared in movies like 3 Strikes as Big Mo, Grow House as Bam, and Wash as Face.

Biography Timeline

Shawn Fonteno is an American actor, rapper, and motion-capture artist best known for his role as Franklin Clinton in the popular video game Grand Theft Auto V. His life has been marked by several significant events, as detailed below.

1968: Shawn Fonteno was born on April 8 in Los Angeles, California.

1987: Shawn formed the hip-hop duo Kam & Solo alongside his cousin and fellow rapper Jason “Kam” Miller.

1991: Kam & Solo released their debut album, “Soul Brothers,” which received critical acclaim.

1993: Shawn made his acting debut with a small role in the film “Menace II Society.”

2004: Shawn appeared in the film “The Wash,” alongside rappers Snoop Dogg and Dr. Dre.

2013: Shawn was cast as Franklin Clinton in Grand Theft Auto V, becoming one of the best-selling video games ever.

2019: Shawn appeared in the film “The Irishman,” directed by Martin Scorsese.

Biography-Age, Cousin

55-year-old Shawn Darnell “Solo” Fonteno was born in South Los Angeles, California, on April 8, 1976. In Watts, Los Angeles, he was reared by his father, Thomas Fonteno. He is of American nationality, while his ethnicity is African American. Young Maylay, an American rapper and the voice actor for protagonist Carl “CJ” Johnson in “Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas,” is his cousin.

He studied at Locke High School. Shawn became a member of an African-American gang until he was shot and killed. He left the band and turned his life around due to the horrific event. Shawn stole vehicles, was a thief, and sold narcotics while in the team.

Career Movies

Shawn has been in several films over his career. He made his film debut in 1996 with “Dead Homiez,” followed by “3 Strikes” in 2000, “The Wash” in 2001 as Face, and “Grow House” in 2017. At one point, he was a part of Kam&Solo, a rap group affiliated with Ice Cube’s Lench Mob Crew.

Shawn is most known for portraying clever street gangster Franklin Clinton in “Grand Theft Auto V,” which earned over $1 billion. Shawn’s voice was also heard as an unknown Grove Street Families member in “Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas” in 2004.

Shawn was requested to be a part of Rockstar’s newest video game by DJ Pooh, a buddy and consultant on “GTA V.” Pooh also authored and co-produced “Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas,” but Fonteno is famous mainly for his role as Red in the film “Friday.”

Personal Life

The American voice-over actor has been married twice in his lifetime. He married Bridgette Johnson on August 9 1996, and has a daughter named Bria with her. Shawn also has a daughter named Brittney from his previous marriage.

Feud with Ice Cube

Shawn had a public business battle with Ice Cube, which he detailed in the documentary “Beef 2.” After a business argument, Shawn knocked Ice Cube unconscious and took his chain. The confrontation is said to have occurred at a traffic signal, with Ice Cube initiating it.
